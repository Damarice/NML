About Northcrest Management Limited

Your Trusted Partner in Consultancy and Advisory Services

Northcrest Management Limited (NML) is a top consultancy firm based in Kenya, incorporated under the Companies Act, 2015. We provide advisory services to individuals, businesses, and organizations, focusing on growth and effective decision-making.

Our goal is to partner with clients for significant improvements, meeting both short-term and long-term objectives with tailored, internationally compliant solutions.

Specializing in Project Management, Strategy Consulting, Transition Management, Business Sustainability, Financial Advisory, and Corporate Training, we equip clients to overcome challenges and achieve success.

We also work with companies and NGOs to execute targeted interventions through corporate social responsibility initiatives, promoting responsible corporate citizenship.

Why Choose NML

Expert Insights into Business & Corporate Practice

NML is the definitive source of expert insights into the complexities of business and corporate practice. Our dedicated team of experts combines decades of experience and expertise in East Africa.

Core Competencies

Clients rely on NML for superior delivery across our four primary competencies, namely research, analysis, synthesis, and reporting.

1. Research

Tried and tested data collection methodologies tailored to the needs of each project. NML specializes in desktop research, interviews, surveys, research panels, and focus groups.

2. Analysis

We specialize in translating research findings into meaningful insights to guide our clients. This includes qualitative and quantitative data analysis, contextualization, and interpretation of data and trends.

3. Synthesis

At NML, synthesis of data into clear and targeted outputs is key. We dissect and refine research findings to ensure final outputs are molded to the clients’ needs.

4. Reporting

In each project, we endeavor to develop reports and briefs in line with client specifications, bearing in mind the length and depth of analysis required.
