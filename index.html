About Northcrest Management Limited

Your Trusted Partner in Consultancy and Advisory Services

Northcrest Management Limited (NML) is a top consultancy firm based in Kenya, incorporated under the Companies Act, 2015. We provide advisory services to individuals, businesses, and organizations, focusing on growth and effective decision-making.

Our goal is to partner with clients for significant improvements, meeting both short-term and long-term objectives with tailored, internationally compliant solutions.

Specializing in Project Management, Strategy Consulting, Transition Management, Business Sustainability, Financial Advisory, and Corporate Training, we equip clients to overcome challenges and achieve success.

We also work with companies and NGOs to execute targeted interventions through corporate social responsibility initiatives, promoting responsible corporate citizenship.

Our Offerings

Expert Consulting Services

Project Management Services

NML partners with enterprises to enhance project outcomes through expert management support. Our services include reviewing processes, identifying weaknesses, recommending improvements, and implementing new tools and procedures. We also offer change management to ensure smooth transitions.

Strategy Consulting

We assist organizations in crafting and implementing strategic plans. Our approach includes collaborating with decision-makers, conducting interviews, and aligning objectives with business goals. We also provide implementation plans to help turn strategies into actionable steps.

Transition Management

NML helps organizations manage internal and external changes. We support redesigning processes, automating workflows, restructuring organizations, and facilitating cultural changes. Our goal is to guide teams through transitions and ensure effective implementation.

Human Capital Consulting

Our consulting services focus on optimizing human resources. We offer solutions for effective talent management, organizational development, and enhancing overall HR effectiveness. We aim to help organizations leverage their workforce as a key asset.

Business Sustainability Support

We assist SMEs with crucial areas for growth and sustainability. Our services include business planning, market feasibility studies, financial management training, customer focus strategies, business intelligence, and process engineering. We help SMEs overcome challenges and thrive.
